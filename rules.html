OTC MeetUp Rules

  • A double COVID vaccination status is mandatory. COVID vaccination certificates will be checked at the venue. (Hard copies are not required.)
  • Masks are recommended.
  • Invites/tickets need to be produced upon entering the venue. (Hard copies are not required.)
  • Please follow the Code of Conduct while attending our meetups. Any violations can be reported to the organisers or on contact@ourtech.community.
