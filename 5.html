OTC MeetUp #5

In-person Tech Meetup

About

We enjoyed informative talks and meaningful conversations with over 70 people at our fourth in-person meetup! We also celebrated completing four years of OTC!

We want to thank everyone who showed up for the meetup and our speakers who imparted knowledge to all of us! We are also grateful to Devfolio for supporting us in conducting this meetup!

Time (IST) Event
3:00 - 2:15 PM Settling in
2:15 - 2:25 PM Opening remarks by Harsh Kapadia on four years of OTC and meetup logistics.

Slide deck
2:30 - 3:00 PM 'Dorking Adventures: That one time on Google... ' by Jaden Furtado

Google Dorking or Google hacking refers to using Google search techniques to hack into vulnerable sites or search for information that is not available in public search results. Yet, it's something that is unknown outside the Cybersecurity/OSINT community! This is a talk about some of the crazy, insane and mind-bending stuff that is out there and explores the possibilities that Google opens up.


Slide deck
3:10 - 3:40 PM 'Showcasing Compiler Code Optimizations' by Kartik Soneji

We will use Godbolt to visualize how GCC optimizes C and C++ code.


Resources and topics: Constant Folding, -fno-builtin, Loop Unrolling, Dead Code Elimination, compileroptimizations.com

3:50 - 4:00 PM Announcements
4:00 - 4:30 PM 'Navigating the CI/CD Landscape' by Darshit Suratwala

Let's dive deeper into "Navigating the CI/CD Landscape" as we explore the basics of automation, showcase real-world examples, and learn how to create your own pipelines with a simple example. Whether you're new to the concept or looking to enhance your skills, this session offers a straightforward guide to mastering CI/CD practices for streamlined and efficient software development.
