About

We successfully hosted our first official in-person (offline) event with 62 attendees!

We all had fun listening to an awesome talk on Community Principles and an insightful panel discussion on Communities, Learning and Progressing in Tech.

We closed off the event with snacks and a few hours of Networking.

We appreciate Himanshu Sharma flying out to meet us and attend the event.
