OTC MeetUp #2

In-person Tech Meetup

About

We successfully hosted our second meetup with 63 attendees!

We had a great time with everyone and we hope you did too! We started the meetup with introduction and some memorable moments from our 100 catchups by Darshan Rander.

We had a great talk on "API Keys, feelings and other vulnerabilities" by Jaden Furtado followed by a talk on "Need for Competitive Programming?" by Saket Thota and a final talk on "Organizational Culture" by Tushar Nankani.

We closed off the event with snacks and a few hours of Networking.
